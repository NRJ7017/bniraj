ABOUT US

Reflux is annual chemical Engineering Symposium of IIT Guwahati ,is organized in the last weak of March every year. It has been a pioneer in chemical industries and entrepreneurial scene of country.

The aim of Reflux is to provide an opportunity for building Chemical Engineers to share scientific expertise and knowledge toward the development of new methods strategies in different fields of Chemical Engineering.The event is planned to be highly interactive and an excellent learning experience for all the delegates.

We have invited lectures, workshops and a wide range of events, exhibitions,and competitions to be held during these three days along with research paper presentation.Over the years ,Reflux has proved itself as a genuine plateform to interact with the youth and engaged them.

So dive into the 5th edition of Reflux and explore this year's theme "Climate Unchanged:For a better tomorrow
